About us

Lollapalooza is a 4-Day music festival happening July 31 - August 3, 2025 at historic Grant Park in Chicago, Illinois, USA.

Lollapalooza is more than just a music festival. It’s a community. It’s a place where you can come as you are and celebrate all the best things in life with people who love them too.
